FoodFighters - Improving Memory Retention of Food Items through a Mobile Serious Game

Description: An application made to reduce food waste via memory retention and gamification techniques.

Tech Used/Competences Gained: Making application in Android Studio. Creating "character" customizor and saving user made creations.

Program briefly explained:

  1. Take picture of food fighter you wish to create.
  2. Crop said photo by dragging finger across entire food item. Confirm when you are happy with crop.
  3. Customize your food fighter with options for hands/feet/hair/eyes/mouth.
  4. When customization is done, give character a name.
  5. Go into 1 on 1 battle with a list of pre-set characters in the application.

Building Teamwork: Mixed Reality Game for Developing Trust and Communication

Description: A mixed relity VR application task digitizing legos from a model in the real world, to a VR application, by the use of image processing and socket streaming.

Tech Used/Competences Gained: Python for image processing, Unity/C# for scene creation of VR application, socket streaming for communication between Python and Unity/C#.

Program briefly explained:

  • VR user has two Lego "statues" in front of them in a puzzle room and needs them to match. However they do not have any legos in pocession, so they have to communicate to their partner.
  • User not in VR is set infront of a lego plane and has to built the figure which the VR player tells them too. Since they are not in VR, they can only rely on verbal information given by the VR player.
  • Once both figures match, a door in the VR application opens and the VR player can progress.
